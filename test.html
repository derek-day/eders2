Popular Flavors

  • ALABASTER COFFEE

    Experience the rich, bold flavor of our coffee ice cream, crafted with fresh brewed roast from Alabaster Coffee!

  • ALABASTER COFFEE N' COOKIES

    Do you like the aromatic notes of our Alabaster Coffee ice cream? How about adding some cookies to it!

  • BANANA

    Smooth and bursting with the natural sweetness of ripe bananas, our Banana ice cream is a refreshing treat.

  • CHERRY GARDEN

    Delight in the sweet and tangy taste of our Cherry ice cream.

  • CHOCOLATE CHIP

    A timeless classic, our Chocolate Chip ice cream combines creamy vanilla with rich chocolate chips.

  • CHOCOLATE CHIP COCONUT

    A delightful twist of our Chocolate Chip ice cream, we add sweet and tart pineapple flavors to give the classic a spin.

  • CHOCOLATE PEANUT BUTTER

    This decadent treat features velvety chocolate ice cream swirled with ribbons of salty-sweet peanut butter.

  • COCONUT DREAM

    A refreshing tropical treat to help take you to the summer a few months early.

  • COOKIE DOUGH

    Our Cookie Dough ice cream is a knock-out with creamy vanilla ice cream, packed with generous chunks of cookie dough bites.

Eder’s has been scooping up smiles since 1927, and doesn’t show any signs of slowing down. Visit for impeccable takes on classic flavors.
